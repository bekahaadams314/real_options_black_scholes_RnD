# Real Options Black Scholes for Pharma R&D

* $S$ (Stock Price) $\rightarrow$ Present Value of Expected Future Cash Flows: The projected market revenue if the drug successfully launches.
* $K$ (Strike Price) $\rightarrow$ Phase III Clinical Trial & Launch Costs: The capital investment required to execute the next phase.
* $t$ (Time to Maturity) $\rightarrow$ Regulatory/Trial Window: The time remaining before the patent exclusivity begins to decay or the trial window closes.
* $\sigma$ (Volatility) $\rightarrow$ Market & Clinical Uncertainty: The volatility of the target therapeutic market size or pricing space.
* $r$ (Risk-free rate) $\rightarrow$ Cost of Capital: The standard macroeconomic risk-free yield.
